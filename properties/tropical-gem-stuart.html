About This Property

Welcome to the Tropical Gem — a charming 2-bedroom, 1.5-bath retreat nestled in one of Stuart's most convenient neighborhoods. With two miles to downtown Stuart and four miles to Stuart Beach, you're never far from the best the area has to offer, whether that's waterfront dining, boutique shopping, or a day on the sand.

Step outside and enjoy your private pool or unwind on one of two private patios surrounded by lush tropical landscaping. Pet-friendly and comfortable for up to six guests, this property is the perfect Florida getaway for families or groups who want a great location without sacrificing privacy or comfort.
