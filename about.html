About Us

Connecting visitors with the best vacation rentals in Stuart, Florida.

Tidemark Vacation Rentals curates hand-picked VRBO and Airbnb properties across Stuart, FL and the surrounding Treasure Coast area. Our listings highlight waterfront homes, family-friendly cottages, and pet-friendly condos — all sourced from trusted platforms.

This site provides quick links to verified postings so you can find and book your perfect getaway without the hassle of sifting through hundreds of results.

Stuart, FL sits along the St. Lucie River and is known as the "Sailfish Capital of the World." Whether you're after the beach, the waterway, or the charming downtown, there's a rental here for you.
